Platform Overview

Tryout is a centralized sports management platform built to streamline every layer of association operations — from first registration to final roster.

  • Digital Player Registration & Payments
  • Tryouts, Evaluations & Live Scoring
  • Team Formation & Roster Management
  • Season Scheduling & Attendance
  • Coach, Parent & Player Communication
→ One system. One login. Full control.

⚠️ The Problem Today

  • 🔀 Too many disconnected tools — spreadsheets, forms, email chains
  • 📝 Manual evaluations with no standardized scoring or rankings
  • 🔁 Repeated data entry every season across multiple platforms
  • 📉 Poor visibility into tryout results, attendance, and team status
  • 💸 Revenue lost to manual payment tracking and no-shows
